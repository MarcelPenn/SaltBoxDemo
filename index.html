The Salt Box Co.

Make yourself at home at The Old Salt Box Co. along Newfoundland and Labrador’s beautiful Atlantic coast. Our fully restored, century Old Salt Box homes offer you a unique, luxury accommodation to call home while you explore the many beauties of the area. We have made it our vision to ensure that your stay in our homes is a memorable part of your vacation. Each home offers fresh contemporary furnishings, modern appliances, authentic cast iron antique tubs and of course our signature picture window.
